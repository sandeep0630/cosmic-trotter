Om (AUM)

The Cosmic Sound – The Essence of All Creation

Key Takeaways

  • Chant with Awareness: When you say Om, feel the vibration in your body and the silence that follows.
  • Start and End with Om: Begin and end your day or meditation with Om to connect with the cosmic rhythm.
  • Remember the Silence: The most important part of Om is the silence after it. That is your true nature.
  • See Everything as Vibration: Modern science says everything is energy vibrating at different frequencies. The ancients called it Om.

Before the universe existed, there was only sound. And that sound was **Om**.

The Sound of the Universe

The **Mandukya Upanishad** declares that Om (AUM) is the essence of all creation. It is not just a chant — it is the **cosmic vibration** from which everything emerged.

The Three Sounds of Om

  • A (अ) – The waking state, the physical world, the beginning of creation.
  • U (उ) – The dream state, the subtle world, the process of preservation.
  • M (م) – The deep sleep state, dissolution, the end of all things.

Beyond these three is the **fourth state** — Turiya — pure consciousness, represented by the silence after Om.

The Profound Message

Om is not just a sound. It is the **bridge** between the manifest and the unmanifest, between the individual and the universal, between time and eternity.
